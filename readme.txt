Diffusion bass model for look-like analyses
This is for understanding what proportion of the market the new apple smartwatch gain will based in the historical data of the older ones.
The analyses is done in R programming language, and the essential libraries are ggplot2, and diffusion.
Sources:
Info about the new product: Apple Watch Ultra 2: The 200 Best Inventions of 2023 | TIME
Historical data source: Global smartwatch shipments market share 2023 | Statista
Information of total number of smart watch users: Insightful Smartwatch Statistics For 2024 (demandsage.com)
As the data was only for proportions, and it is also interesting to understand the scaling of the sales, total number of users are used as well to make it convertible.
